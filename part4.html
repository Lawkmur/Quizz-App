TOEIC Practice - Part 3: Short Conversations

In this part of the test you will listen to a short conversation between a man and a woman. After the conversation, you will answer three questions about the dialogue. There will be four possible answers for each question. Typical questions include, who, what, where, when, why, and how. You may also be asked to make an inference

Question 50-52

50. Why is the man talking to the woman?

51. Why does the man decide not to see Dr. Coleman the next morning as planned?

52. What will the man most likely do?

Question 53-55

53. What is the woman concerned about?

54. What does the woman say she will do?

55. What does the man ask the woman to do?

Question 56-58

56. What type of business does the man most likely work for?

57. What is the woman worried about?

58. What does the woman need to do before returning the car?

Question 41-43

41. What problem did the delivery man face?

42. What time is the man calling?

44. What does the woman complain about?

Question 44-46

44. What does the woman complain about?

45. What does the man suggest?

46. What does the man say he will do?
