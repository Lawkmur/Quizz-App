ADVICES

Focus in WH Questions

Because in that way will be easier for you to guess the answer. That is why during the test you can´t get distracted.

Expand your Vocabulary

Try to get familiar with vocabulary as much as you can. Otherwise you will not understand

Wrong answers

While you are listening, try to eliminate the answers you think are incorrect. You can use either your fingers or do it mentally.

Trust your instincts

Don´t waste your time changing your mind while answering a question, move on. Trust always in what you heard.

Create sentences

Before the audio is played, try to create sentences and look for vocabulary familiar to the photo.

Find keywords

Find keywords in the question beforehand, and focus in those words. Hear carefully and try you answer fast.

ADITIONAL MATERIAL

Homophones


Homophones are words that are pronounced the same way but have different meanings. Sometimes they’re spelled identically and sometimes they aren’t.
When you’re learning a new language, homophones can be tricky. They can even be confusing if you’re a primary speaker of a language, especially when you’re listening

Vocabulary


Boosting vocabulary is importance in the process of learning English. Especially when it comes with TOEIC.
A strong English vocabulary is even more important if you're preparing to take the TOEIC Exam. Because every single question on the test will measure your understanding of English vocabulary words.
